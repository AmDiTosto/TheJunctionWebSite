Our Menu

Klondike Koffee

Step back in time with our Klondike Koffee, a tribute to the adventurous spirit of the gold rush. This robust blend offers a rich, full-bodied flavor, with each sip revealing the indulgent undertones of caramel and the smooth essence of chocolate. Meticulously crafted to awaken your senses, Klondike Koffee is the perfect companion for both quiet reflections and spirited conversations. Enjoy a cup and savor a piece of history.

Yukon Espresso

Embrace the brisk, fresh energy of the North with our Yukon Espresso. A robust, powerful espresso that captures the essence of the untamed wilderness. Each shot delivers a rich, full-flavored experience, meticulously crafted to invigorate your mornings or energize your afternoons. With its bold aroma and intense flavor profile, our Yukon Espresso is the ideal kick-start for adventurers and dreamers alike, bringing the vigor of the Yukon's natural beauty into every cup.

Northern Lights Latte

Immerse yourself in the enchantment of the aurora borealis with our Northern Lights Latte. This captivating beverage artfully combines rich espresso with perfectly steamed milk, creating a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. Each layer dances like the ethereal lights of the northern sky, offering a visual and sensory delight. Ideal for moments of relaxation or contemplation, our Northern Lights Latte promises a magical coffee experience, reminiscent of a clear, starlit night under the mystical aurora.

Dawson City Cappuccino

Immerse yourself in the warmth of our Dawson City Cappuccino, a harmonious blend that pays homage to the bustling spice trade of the Klondike era. Each cup is expertly crafted with rich, velvety foam, infused with the finest espresso, and delicately topped with a dusting of cinnamon and nutmeg. This aromatic creation is not just a drink, but a journey through time, evoking the spirited essence of Dawson City. Perfect for cozy moments or as an inspiring start to your day, our cappuccino is a testament to timeless flavors and the spirit of exploration.

Sourdough Scones

Delight in the exquisite taste of our artisanal sourdough scones, a perfect blend of a delicate sour tang and a heavenly buttery feel. These scones are a testament to the art of baking, golden-brown on the outside with a wonderfully flaky crust, and irresistibly soft within. They make for an elegant breakfast or a charming teatime indulgence. Enjoy them with your choice of preserves, clotted cream, or savor their pure flavor solo.

Aurora Apple Pie

Discover the enchantment of our Aurora Apple Pie, where a flaky, golden crust embraces a generous spiced apple filling. The warmth of cinnamon and nutmeg weaves a delightful tapestry of flavors, harmonizing perfectly with the sweet-tart apples. With each bite, you'll be transported to a cozy autumn evening, and whether you choose to savor it as is or pair it with a scoop of velvety vanilla ice cream, this pie promises a dessert experience that's simply heavenly.

Midnight Sun Macarons

Embark on a culinary journey through the Yukon with our Midnight Sun Macarons. These delightful treats come in a variety of flavors, including wild blueberry, spruce tip, cranberry, cloudberry, and fireweed honey. Each macaron is a miniature masterpiece, capturing the essence of the vibrant natural beauty of the midnight sun. Whether you're drawn to the sweet-tart notes of cranberry or the unique allure of spruce tip, these bite-sized indulgences promise a taste of the Yukon's finest offerings, making them the perfect accompaniment to your day.
